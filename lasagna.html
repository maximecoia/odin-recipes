Lasagna

Description

This classic lasagna recipe is a hearty dish layered with rich meat sauce, creamy ricotta, and melted mozzarella cheese. It’s perfect for a family dinner or a cozy gathering with friends.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Egg
  • Garlic
  • Italian seasoning

Steps

  1. Boil the lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  2. In a pan, cook the ground beef with garlic and Italian seasoning, then add tomato sauce.
  3. Mix ricotta cheese with an egg and some Parmesan cheese.
  4. Layer the noodles, meat sauce, ricotta mixture, and mozzarella cheese in a baking dish.
  5. Repeat the layers and top with more mozzarella and Parmesan.
  6. Bake at 375°F for 30 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and golden.
